Killer Carousel Example: Using events with carousel items

Adding your own functionality to carousel items is quite easy.

Before initialising Killer Carousel, bind your desired events and handlers to the carousel items.

When Killer Carousel is started, it will automatically carry over your events and handlers so they function in the carousel.

In this example, when clicking or mouse-over the items, the contents of the image alt attribute are copied into a div in the top left corner.
